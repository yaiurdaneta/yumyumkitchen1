Yum Yum Kitchen Fresh food done right

Ingredients

  • 1-2 Tablespoons Olive Oil
  • 1/2 red bell pepper chopped
  • 1/2 green bell pepper chopped
  • 1/2 yellow pepper chopped
  • 1 medium onion chopped
  • 1 clove garlic minced
  • 2-3 cups jasmine or basmati rice cooked
  • 1 teaspoon tomato paste
  • 1 cup chickpeas
  • salt to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 small tomato chopped
  • parsley garnish

Mediterranean rice cooking instructions

  1. In a large skillet saute the peppers, onions, and garlic with olive oil.
  2. Once tender add in the rice, tomato paste, and chickpeas.
  3. Mix all together and add paprika, salt to taste.
  4. Top with fresh chopped tomatoes and garnish with parsley.
